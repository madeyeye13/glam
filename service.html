OUR FOUNDER

Meet The Founder

Opeyemi Ayeni is the founder and creative force behind Glam1907 Events, a company known for delivering exceptional event experiences. With a background in event planning and a passion for bringing visions to life, Opeyemi has built a reputation for her attention to detail, creativity, and personalized approach to every event.

Her journey in the event industry began with a desire to create memorable occasions that reflect her clients' unique personalities and needs. Over the years, she has successfully planned and coordinated a wide range of events, from weddings and corporate functions to intimate gatherings. Opeyemi’s ability to manage complex logistics while maintaining a calm and organized presence has made her a go-to professional in the industry.

At Glam1907 Events, Opeyemi leads a dedicated team, ensuring each event is flawlessly executed, from concept to completion. Her commitment to excellence and passion for creating seamless experiences has earned her the trust and loyalty of her clients, making Glam1907 Events a top choice for those seeking truly unforgettable events.
